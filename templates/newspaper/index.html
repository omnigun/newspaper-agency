About Control System

One day, the editor-in-chief of our newspaper, Mr.Better, had a wonderful idea:
"I have a wonderful team of editors. However, I do not have time to track the newspapers that the agency publishes properly."
So Mr.Better decided to create this system that allows you to track the editors responsible for each issue of the newspaper. This allows you to always know exactly who is responsible for each newspaper. And it also saves your nerves.
